The $999 Vision

$999 was conceived as a way to bring awareness to mental health and substance addiction issues that affect millions of people. We want to provide an enviroment that can encourage charitable giving to help those who are struggling at the end of the day.

The idea of this project is to slightly change the memecoin narrative on Solana. Our team is sick of seeing meaningless projects achieve incedible milestones without giving back to the world, which is why we have structured the tokenomics in a way that will make donating over $200,000 to LiveFree999 possible.

While $999 is not affiliated with LiveFree999, we have shared values and a desire to help make a positive impact.

Mental health isn't something that get's talked about regularly within the Solana ecosystem, so we're here to change that!
